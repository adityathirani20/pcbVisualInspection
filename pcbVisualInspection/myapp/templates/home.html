Welcome to PCB Assembly Inspection!

Our company specializes in providing high-quality PCB assembly inspection services for businesses and individuals alike. With years of experience and a team of highly skilled technicians, we are committed to ensuring the quality and reliability of your PCB assemblies.

Our Services

Pre-Assembly Inspection

Our pre-assembly inspection services help to identify any issues with your components or PCB layout before assembly. This can save time and money by catching potential issues early in the process.

In-Assembly Inspection

Our in-assembly inspection services help to ensure the quality and reliability of your PCB assemblies during the assembly process. This includes visual inspection, X-ray inspection of BGA and other components, and functional testing.

Post-Assembly Inspection

Our post-assembly inspection services help to identify any issues with your completed PCB assemblies before they are shipped. This can help to prevent costly returns or repairs.
